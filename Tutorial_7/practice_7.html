Practice 7¶

Question 1¶

Follow the steps below to create a 3 x 5 numpy array representing the following data:

\[\begin{split}\begin{matrix} 0 & 1 & 2 & 3 & 4 \\ 0 & 1.5 & 3 & 4.5 & 6\\ 0 & 0.25 & 0.5 & 0.75 & 1 \end{matrix}\end{split}\]

  1. Use np.linspace or np.arange to create three 1 x 5 arrays [0 1 2 3 4], [0. 1.5 3. 4.5 6.] and [0 0.25 0.5 0.75 1.].

  2. Use np.zeros to create an 5 x 3 array of zeros.

  3. Use slicing to assign each 1 x 5 array to each row of the array.
